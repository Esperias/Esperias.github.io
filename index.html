How to Play

Please select your platform to see the instructions!

  1. Press Win+R (Windows Key + R).
  2. Type in C:\Windows\System32\drivers\etc and press Enter.
  3. Right-click on the "hosts" file and open it with Notepad.
  4. Add the lines from the box on the right to the bottom of the file.
  5. Save the file. You may need administrator permissions.
  6. Open Growtopia and Connect!

We recommend using Hosts Go for the easiest setup.

  1. Download and Install HostsGo APK.
  2. Open Hosts Go and click on "Hosts Editor".
  3. Click the checkbox on the right, and click "Download Hosts File".
  4. Enter this URL: https://esperias.github.io/hosts.txt
  5. Click "Download and Apply".
  6. Return to the main screen and click "START".
  7. Open Growtopia and connect!

Alternative apps: Virtual Host, PowerTunnel.

For iOS, you will need the Surge 5 App to connect.

  1. Download "Surge 5" from the App Store.
  2. Press "OK".
  3. Click on "Default.conf".
  4. Click on "IMPORT" part - Download Profile from URL.
  5. Put the URL from your copy when you click the iOS host button above and click "OK" and "Done".
  6. Press "SETUP" and then agree to policy by clicking "OK" and "Allow" for VPN Configuration.
  7. Done, then open Growtopia and Connect!
  1. Open Finder and, in the Finder menu bar, select Go > Go to Folder.
  2. Type /private/etc/hosts in the textbox and press Return.
  3. Move the 'hosts' file to your Desktop.
  4. Open the hosts file, and add the IP from the box on the right.
  5. Save the file and return it to its original location.
  6. Open Growtopia and Connect!

About Esperia

The Private Server, Reimagined.

Welcome to Esperia, a thriving community with over 19,000 members. More than just a game, it’s a space where creativity and exploration come to life.

With a strong moderation and a zero-tolerance policy for disruptive behavior, players can enjoy the game without worry, focusing on building, learning, and connecting with others. The server continues to grow thanks to its dedicated community.

Server Rules

Be Respectful

Treat all players and staff with kindness and respect. No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.

No Cheating

The use of third-party programs, hacks, or exploits is strictly forbidden.

No Scamming

Do not attempt to deceive or steal from other players. All trades are final.

No Spamming

Avoid spamming chat with repetitive messages, advertising, or excessive capitalization.

Listen to Staff

Follow the instructions of server moderators and administrators at all times.

Keep it PG

Keep conversations and world content appropriate for all ages.
